My goal as a teacher is to develop a total child. I believe that fostering on students' mental health, academic development, cognitive development, social-emotional development, physical health and identity development will help them to meet the demand of the 21s Century. I teach using interactive based learning activities. These learning activities enable students to collaborate, think critically, analyse, solve problems, and be innovative as well as creative. They become change makers who are able to bring positive changes in their communities rather than passive consumers who just passively observe what is going on in the society. The use of technology must be embraced and integrated in my classroom. I cherish and encourage freedom of expression in my classroom.

Contributions To Students' Development

I am a multi-talented teacher and a high achiever. I do make my physical and virtual classrooms more innovative and creative. This I extend to other subjects in sclence and mathematics department. For example, I created instructional games to teach water cycle, Nitrogen cycle in Biology and Atomic structure in Chemistry to enhance deep understanding of these concepts among the students. I created instructional material to teach Trigonometric ratio in Mathematics by using fingers. Some instructional materials in Physics were modeled by my students to let them have deep understanding of the concept taught. These creative instructional materials were presented at 2021 Lagos State Festhwal of Instructional Material; the project won 2nd position in Lagos State, Project category.
In addition, my creative and innovative skills enable me to create a craftwork for 2021 Lagos State Tree Planting Day. My project came 1st in Lagos State and Botanical Garden was donated to the school by Lagos State Parks and Garden Agency (LASPARK) and Nigeria Conservation Foundation. When I was at Ojota Senior Secondary School, my projects, pot-in-pot refrigerator and ethanol stove won the best project award during 2014 climate change. My student was sponsored to Germany by Lagos State Govemment. Even as a Physics teacher, I groomed students for all the competitions in the school such as drama, debate, Spelling-Bee and science competitions. I am responsible for coordinating co-curricular activities within the school. Many awards were won for the school at both State and National levels. Through my unrelented efforts some of my students have secured scholarships to study at university. Also, my student was sponsored to Finland by Lagos State Government for being the second runner-up in 2014 Final Lagos State Spelling Bee competition when I was at Ojota Senior Secondary School.
Furthermore, my students have been consistently recording 100% performance in Physics at both WAEC and NECO. They were able to develop critical thinking, collaboration, digital, creativity and problem solving skills as a result of the inquiry-based learning activities use in my classroom. This helped and enabled them to compete with undergraduates from universities in Designathon 2020 4 Youth By Youth contest. Out of 264 research proposals that were submitted for the competition throughout Nigeria, the research proposal from my students (Edoro Team) was among 13 entries that were selected for final contest at Designathon 2020. My students (Edoro Team) from Kosofe Senior College came up with 3rd position in Nigeria at the final contest. University of Ibadan came up with 1st position and University of llorin was the 1 runner-up. They were given cash prizes.
Also, the good relationship I have with my sutdents enabled me to detect an indigent student who was kidnapped from Benin Republic when he was 9 years old. He could not remember anything about his family again. Since then, he has been sleeping in front of shops at New Garage, Ojota. He worked at Owode after school hours to take care of himself. I presented his case to an NGO known as Human Physics Department at Obafemi Awolowo University Ile-lfe. The parents will not know that their child is still alive, but l believe God can still connect him with his family one day. Above all, a student was withdrawn from Lagos State Model College, lgbobo in 2004 because he repeated twice and was brought to Kosofe Senior College under my supervision as his class teacher, through proper monitoring and counseling, the boy had the best WASSCE result in Kosofe Zone, Lagos as a Medical Doctor, now in abroad. I am multi-tasking, that is, I can work on several activities at the same time with or without supervision such that none will suffer. I have not become Vice-Principal but because we have only one Vice-Principal in the school, I perform some roles of vice principal in the school.
